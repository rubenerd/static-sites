About

Her background is both Australian and German, though she spent many of her formative years growing up in South East Asia. During her time at UTS, Elke was an active volunteer in the Peer Networking program assisting new students during Orientation, and she remains a proud member of the Golden Key Society.

Elke is a freelance writer and editor, as well as a hobbyist photographer. Her repertoire of interests also includes fashion, food, feminism, politics, and fitness.
