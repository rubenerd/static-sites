Product Review: K-Palette 1 Day Tattoo

It is no secret that I have struggled with many, many different types of eyeliner. I have not been blessed with a steady hand, or flawless skin, and I will admit that there have been many mornings in my past where I have had to wipe an embarrassing mess off from my eyelids and start again in a fit of frustration. That is, until a friend recommended that I try K-Palette’s 1 Day Tattoo eyeliner. At only SGD$20 from any beauty and skincare outlet (such as Watsons or Sasa) it has all the elements of a liquid liner—quality, strength, permanence—without the normal, fiddly brush applicator. Instead, K-Palette has designed their eyeliner to act more like a very fine felt-tip pen with a soft, flexible end. It is easy to apply, dries almost instantly, and lasts all day—even on oily skin in Singapore’s 30 degree heat. It appears as though my seemingly endless search for an eyeliner that covers all the bases has finally come to a close!
